My Message to You :

In creating this encyclopedia, my goal is to also share my insights and experiences with other collectors of receipts as well as curious readers.Through exploring the complexities of collecting receipts, navigating through many return and refund policies of retailers, and examining the underlying psychology of consumer behavior, I hope that this encyclopedia will serve as a valuable resource for anyone seeking to understand the complexities of collecting receipts and the implications it can have on our lives.

By the time I didn't realize, I found myself having an enormous pile of receipts in front of my computer desk. From the simplest purchases to the most significant transactions, I was actually meticulously saving every piece of paper that documented my spending. For as long as I can remember, I've had a habit of collecting receipts -- a habit that has often been met with bemusement by friends.

As the author of this website, I find myself drawn to the habit of collecting receipts, often storing them away for safekeeping, just in case I might need them for a return or refund. Over time, this habit evolved into a comprehensive collection, prompting me to explore more about receipts.
