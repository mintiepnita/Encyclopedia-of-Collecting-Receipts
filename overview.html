From Barcode to Thermal Print

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

• Scanning the Barcode:

Items are scanned at the checkout counter using a barcode scanner, quickly transmitting data to the point-of-sale (POS) system.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

• Cashier Machine Processing :

The POS system on the cashier machine processes scanned data, retrieving item details, prices, and discounts.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

• Generating the Receipt Data :

The POS system generates receipt content, including purchased items, prices, taxes, and the total amount due.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

• Data Sent to Printer :

Receipt data is sent to the thermal receipt printer for printing, utilizing efficient thermal printing technology.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

• Thermal Printing :

The printer transfers ink onto heat-sensitive paper, producing clear prints of text, graphics, and barcodes without ink cartridges.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

• Cutting & Presenting the Receipt :

After the receipt is printed, the printer's cutter mechanism automatically cuts the paper to the appropriate length. The cashier then presents the printed receipt to the customer along with their purchased items, providing a complete record of the transaction.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

This process ensures customers get accurate receipts promptly.
